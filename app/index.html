Better Ballard

Better Ballard is a community-driven organization and marketing capaign to drive action in picking up trash around Ballard. Our goal is to make Ballard completely litter-free. We are doing this by:

  1. calling on individuals of Ballard to play their part in the gradual pickup of trash
  2. organizing recurring litter patrol groups to pick up trash (inspired by Green Lake Litter Patrol)
If this sounds like a future and neighborhood you want, please consider volunteering your time.
