Better Ballard is a community-driven meetup group and awareness campaign to help make Ballard the best it can be. Our current goal is to make Ballard completely litter-free. We are doing this by:

  1. calling on individuals of Ballard to play their part in the gradual pickup of litter
  2. organizing recurring meetup groups to pick up litter
We will expand our scope beyond litter once that problem is sufficiently resolved :)
If this sounds like a future and neighborhood you want, please consider volunteering your time.
